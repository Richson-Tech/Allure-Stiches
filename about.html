About Us

Allure Stitches is a professional unisex tailoring service. The goal of Allure Stitches is to redefine tailoring in Nigeria by providing unique, efficient, satisfactory and above all timely service at a slim budget friendly using latest technologies and following latest trends in a very creative environment. Do you have your own design you'd like to make into an outfit? Can't find any design you'd like or do not have any design in mind? Don't worry, we have your back always!. We offer the cafeteria approach from fabric selection to design selection and outfit Production. We are always able to interpret your designs and make it into that fabulous outfit you desire. If you don't have any design we are able to make suggestions for you.

Allure Stitches is a Nigerian fashion design company that started full operation in 2019. We specialise in designing and making unisex, bespoke and ready-to-wear outfits for the classy and modest clients. We also design and produce kids clothing as well, hand-made fashion accessories. We have a reputation for delivering neatly sewn and well-tailored garments in a timely workframe. In addition to our tailoring and fashion design services, we have a well-structured training which is a system designed to empower young people and mentor them in establishing and building their own businesses. Our vision is to be a brand that provides quality clothing that will meet local demands while also competing with foreign fashion brands. We seek to create clothes that combine simplicity with elegance and uniqueness all within a practical/realistic budget.
