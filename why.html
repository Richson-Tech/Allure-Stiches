Why Us

Why Us?

We aim at making our customers very satisfied with our work. We provide open channels for communication during and after a cloth is made.Allure Stitches is a professional unisex tailoring service. The goal of Allure Stitches is to redefine tailoring in Nigeria by providing unique, efficient, satisfactory and above all timely service at a slim budget friendly using latest technologies and following latest trends in a very creative environment. Do you have your own design you'd like to make into an outfit? Can't find any design you'd like or do not have any design in mind? Don't worry, we have your back always!. We offer the cafeteria approach from fabric selection to design selection and outfit Production. We are always able to interpret your designs and make it into that fabulous outfit you desire. If you don't have any design we are able to make suggestions for you.

Efficient Skill

We always strive to give the best in materials used, sewing, finishing, and packaging. We don't cut corners to save cost but go all out to give you your monies worth.

Afoordable

We can boldly say that we give the best price for the quality of service rendered. We leave our prices open to show that.

Timely

We are here to change the perception of tailors being late. We always stick to time given in everything we do.
